Recurrence

Shortcuts — how to enter recurrence

Type a shortcut into the task field (with a month selected) or into the date field of an existing item. You can combine several rules in a row.

r1m r2mevery / every 2nd month
r4w r10d r1yevery 4 weeks · 10 days · 1 year
r12.mthe 12th of every month
r12.m r24.mthe 12th and 24th of every month (combined)
r06 r6 r12every year in the given month (6 = June)
r24.7.every year on 24.7.
rmo rwe rtuday of the week — the ghost lists the occurrence days in the month
r3sa rlsuthe 3rd Saturday · the last Sunday of every month (1–5 or l = last)

The month of entry = anchor. The rule shows in that month and onward per the rule as a dashed "ghost" (🔃). Click 🔃 = confirm into the month, ✕ = skip that month.
